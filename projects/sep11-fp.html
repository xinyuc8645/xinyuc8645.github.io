Product of Our Game

Alright, so product of what me and my partner made. Alright so I'm going befoe diving in, I just want to say for the project I mainly serve as the frontend of the whole project, while my partner mostly worked on the backend. So basically our game had multiple level's with 3 levels. Additionally, since our theme is focused mostly around "hardcore" the first couple 2 levels may seem "easy" but trust me once you encounter the third and final level, you'll learn how hard the level is. We also had an planning of how our design for the map is going to look like; for example our planning layout for level 1 is simple, look below for reference

We mainly in-corparated things that would normally appear in an normal dungeon game. For example we had many choice of choosing what our character/sprite was going to look like. I'm not going to lie me and my partner are extremely picky about what the sprite/character look like. HOWEVER since the genre revolves around dungeon we decided we would make the sprite an knight, and named our game the "knight and his journey". The basic goal and premise of our dungeon game is simple You use the keyboard keys d for moving right and using the a key for moving to the left, and w key for jumping up. No s key for jumping though or pulling you do though. You have to move the knight character to jump on platform's, collect the coins required and collect key to unlock the doors.

Then when you encounter the brickwall you have to answer a question base on the level. For example since its level 1 the questions are easy. Whereas compare it to level 3, it is much harder than level 1. And also there is an live system, with the player have 3 lives at total. If they touch the spike they lose one live and goes back to the beginning of the level. HOWEVER if they lose all 3 live, they get completely restarted from which level they are currently in. Meaning let's say if they are in level 3 they lose all live, then they died and gets teleport back to all the way at beginning of level 1. That's why I say its hard and tedious. And pretty much that's the product of the dungeon game. Since me and my parnter created this game in order to cure boredom and for those who are up to an challenge.

TakeAways

Procesnation, communication. I mainly want to emphaize these two takeaways. Procesnation causes --> delay and postponing stuff which will make you form an habit of doing stuff in the last minute or so which is not a good habit, and I've done this way too much times. I cannot say this enough, but in life whenever someone assigns you an work do it immediately or get an headstart while you can getting an headstart is better than doing nothing. Since doing it in the last minute or some will causes stress for invdiduals and make them panic which isn't a good thing. So don't procesnate even if you don't know how to do something. Just do it! Communication is also one of the next key things as, an group project like the one I did for SEP11 requires communication with my partner. Without communicating with my partner, I think we would of got this work done in time. So always keep up to date with your partner if you are involved in group work and communicate with them atleast once or twice a week to keep things in check.
